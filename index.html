Welcome here！The Rao Research Group is part of School of Electric Power Engineering at China University of Mining and Technology. Our research is focused on new energy, energy storage, phase change heat transfer, micro/nano scale heat transfer and power battery thermal management technologies, aiming to improve the energy storage density, control the temperature of high power devices and save energy.
